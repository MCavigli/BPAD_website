About What is BPAD?

  1. Home
  2. About

BPAD provides your agency with a vast collection of first-person video scenarios. Each video depicts a situation the candidate might experience on the job. You choose which videos you'd like to use then send them an invitation to participate through their email.

Once the candidate receives the link, they watch the video and record their response through their computer's webcam. This response is then sent back to you for grading.

Creating, sending, and scoring tests is a breeze. BPAD's intuitive design let's you focus on what's important: finding the right person for your agency.
